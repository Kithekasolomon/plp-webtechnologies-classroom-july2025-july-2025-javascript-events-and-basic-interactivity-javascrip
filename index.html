FAQ

How do I borrow a book?
Register as a member and use your member ID to check out books at the library counter.
What is the loan period?
Books can be borrowed for up to 14 days, with an option to renew once.
Can I return books late?
Late returns incur a fine of $1 per day. Please return books on time.
